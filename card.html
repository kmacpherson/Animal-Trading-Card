Wolverine

The largest member of the Mustelidae (weasel) family

  • Scientific Name: Gulo Gulo
  • Habitat: Artic/Sub Artic
  • Diet: Omnivorous
  • Risk: Eastern Population is endangered.

The wolverine is the ultimate surivor in the harsh artic. With fur that is hydrophobic to keep it dry and frost free; To an agressiveness that can back off animals much larger then itself.
